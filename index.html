About South Jeddah Noor Plant

South Jeddah Noor Solar PV IPP is a 300MWac solar power plant that will supply electricity to over 14,000 households. The project supports Saudi Arabia's National Renewable Energy Program and Vision 2030 goals for sustainable development.
